Our Story

WHERE THE OCEAN
MEETS BELHAR.

Sea Night was built on one idea — that great seafood, real Indian cooking, fresh sushi, and proper milkshakes shouldn't be a luxury. Right here in Erica Square, we bring the ocean to your doorstep at prices that make sense.

Whether it's a quick parcel of fish and chips or the full Sea Night Platter for the whole family, every order gets the same care. Fast? Yes. Shortcuts? Never.
